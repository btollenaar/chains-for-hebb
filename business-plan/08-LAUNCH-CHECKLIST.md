# 08 - Launch Checklist

**Last Updated:** February 12, 2026

---

## Pre-Launch: Weeks 1-4

### Week 1: Legal & Foundation

**Priority: Get the business entity established**

- [ ] **Choose brand name** (finalize from options in [07-BRAND-DEVELOPMENT.md](07-BRAND-DEVELOPMENT.md))
  - Check .com domain availability
  - Check social media handle availability (IG, Pinterest, TikTok)
  - Search USPTO trademark database
- [ ] **Register domain** (Namecheap or Cloudflare Registrar)
- [ ] **File LLC** with chosen state
  - Complete Articles of Organization
  - Receive confirmation/certificate
- [ ] **Apply for EIN** on IRS.gov (free, instant)
- [ ] **Open business bank account** (Mercury recommended)
  - Need: EIN + Articles of Organization
- [ ] **Draft Operating Agreement** (single-member LLC template)
- [ ] **Set up professional email** (hello@brand.com, support@brand.com)
  - Google Workspace ($6/mo) or Zoho Mail (free tier)

**Time estimate: 6-8 hours**

### Week 2: Supplier Setup & Product Sampling

**Priority: Establish supplier relationships and order test products**

- [ ] **Create CJDropshipping account** (cjdropshipping.com)
  - Browse eco-friendly product catalog
  - Identify 15-20 potential products
  - Check US warehouse availability
- [ ] **Create Printful account** (printful.com)
  - Set up store connection
  - Design initial branded products (tote bag, care cards)
- [ ] **Order product samples** (10-15 products)
  - Test quality personally
  - Take initial product photos while you have samples
  - Budget: $150-250
- [ ] **Research 3-5 backup suppliers** on Alibaba/Faire
  - Contact for wholesale pricing
  - Request catalogs and MOQ information
- [ ] **Create product spreadsheet** tracking:
  - Product name, SKU, wholesale cost, target retail price
  - Margin calculation, weight, supplier
  - Fulfillment provider assignment

**Time estimate: 8-12 hours**

### Week 3: Store Setup & Content Creation

**Priority: Get the website functional with initial products**

- [ ] **Set up new repository** (clone/fork from template)
  - Configure for new brand (colors, logo, business name)
  - Update `config/business.php` with new business info
  - Configure domain and hosting (DigitalOcean, Hetzner, or DreamHost)
- [ ] **Configure Stripe live account**
  - Complete identity verification
  - Add business information (LLC name, EIN)
  - Connect business bank account
  - Set up webhooks for new domain
  - Test with $1 transaction
- [ ] **Add initial products** (15-25 items)
  - Product titles optimized for SEO
  - Descriptions written in brand voice
  - Professional-quality photos (sample photos from Week 2)
  - Prices set per strategy in [01-PRODUCT-STRATEGY.md](01-PRODUCT-STRATEGY.md)
  - Categories organized
- [ ] **Configure shipping settings**
  - Flat rate: $4.99-5.99 (under $45)
  - Free shipping threshold: $45+
  - Canadian shipping rates
- [ ] **Set up coupon codes**
  - WELCOME10 (10% off first order, for email signup)
  - LAUNCH15 (15% off, launch week promo)
- [ ] **Create initial blog posts** (3-5 posts)
  - "Our Story: Why We Started [Brand]"
  - "5 Easy Eco Swaps for Your Kitchen"
  - "The Complete Guide to Beeswax Wraps"
  - "Best Pour-Over Coffee Setup for Beginners"
  - Use for SEO and social sharing

**Time estimate: 15-20 hours**

### Week 4: Legal Pages, Integrations & Pre-Launch

**Priority: Finalize compliance and tracking**

- [ ] **Publish legal pages** (see [04-LEGAL-SETUP.md](04-LEGAL-SETUP.md))
  - Privacy Policy
  - Terms of Service
  - Return & Refund Policy
  - Shipping Policy
- [ ] **Set up Google Analytics 4**
  - Create GA4 property
  - Add tracking code to layout
  - Configure e-commerce events
  - Link to Google Search Console
- [ ] **Set up Meta Pixel**
  - Create pixel in Meta Business Manager
  - Add pixel code to layout
  - Verify events firing correctly
- [ ] **Set up Google Merchant Center**
  - Create account
  - Verify website ownership
  - Submit product feed (XML)
  - Configure shipping settings
- [ ] **Create social media accounts**
  - Instagram (business account)
  - Pinterest (business account, claim website, enable Rich Pins)
  - TikTok (business account)
  - Facebook Page (for Meta ads)
- [ ] **Set up email capture**
  - Welcome popup/banner with 10% off incentive
  - Newsletter signup in footer (already built)
  - Create welcome email sequence (3 emails)
- [ ] **General liability insurance**
  - Get quotes from Hiscox or Next Insurance
  - Purchase policy
- [ ] **Test complete checkout flow**
  - Place test order on live site
  - Verify Stripe payment processing
  - Verify order confirmation email
  - Verify fulfillment notification works
  - Test on mobile devices
- [ ] **Soft launch to friends/family**
  - Share with 10-20 people
  - Ask for feedback on UX, products, pricing
  - Get initial 3-5 orders for confidence

**Time estimate: 12-16 hours**

## Soft Launch: Weeks 5-8

### Week 5: Social Media Launch

**Priority: Start building audience and social proof**

- [ ] **Instagram launch**
  - Post 5-7 initial posts (product shots, lifestyle, brand story)
  - Write optimized bio with link
  - Follow 50-100 accounts in eco/sustainable niche
  - Engage genuinely (comment, like, share)
  - Start posting 1 Reel per week
- [ ] **Pinterest launch**
  - Create 7-10 boards (see [03-MARKETING-STRATEGY.md](03-MARKETING-STRATEGY.md))
  - Pin 10-15 initial pins per board
  - Start daily pinning (3-5 pins/day)
  - Enable Product Pins
- [ ] **Start content calendar**
  - Schedule 1 week of content in advance
  - Use Canva for graphics
  - Batch-create content (photograph multiple products in one session)
- [ ] **Engage in communities**
  - Reddit: r/ZeroWaste, r/SustainableLiving, r/BuyItForLife
  - Facebook Groups: Eco-friendly living groups
  - Don't spam - add value first, share store only when relevant

**Time estimate: 8-10 hours**

### Week 6: Paid Ads Launch

**Priority: Start generating traffic with small budget**

- [ ] **Launch Google Shopping campaign**
  - Start with Smart Shopping (let Google optimize)
  - Budget: $5-10/day
  - All products in single campaign initially
  - Set up conversion tracking
- [ ] **Monitor and optimize daily**
  - Check CPC, CTR, conversion rate
  - Pause products with high CPC and no conversions after 7 days
  - Note which products get clicks (signals demand)
- [ ] **Start blog posting schedule**
  - 1-2 posts per week
  - Focus on long-tail keywords
  - Include internal links to products
  - Share each post on Pinterest (create 3-5 pins per post)
- [ ] **Email marketing**
  - Send first newsletter to initial subscribers
  - Theme: Brand intro + featured products
  - Monitor open rate and click rate

**Time estimate: 6-8 hours/week**

### Week 7: Optimize & Iterate

**Priority: Act on initial data**

- [ ] **Review analytics** (first 2 weeks of data)
  - Which products get the most views?
  - Which products get added to cart?
  - Where are people dropping off?
  - What traffic sources convert best?
- [ ] **Product page optimization**
  - Improve descriptions for top-viewed products
  - Add more/better photos for products with low conversion
  - Adjust pricing if needed (A/B test)
- [ ] **Request reviews**
  - Email customers who've received orders
  - Ask for product reviews on site
  - Ask for social media shares/tags
- [ ] **Content doubling**
  - Write 2 more blog posts targeting high-intent keywords
  - Create product comparison content
  - Pin consistently (build Pinterest momentum)

**Time estimate: 6-8 hours/week**

### Week 8: Scale What Works

**Priority: Identify winners and double down**

- [ ] **Google Shopping optimization**
  - Segment campaigns by product category
  - Increase budget on high-ROAS products
  - Pause underperformers
  - Optimize product titles and descriptions in feed
- [ ] **Social media growth**
  - Analyze which content types perform best
  - Create more of what works
  - Start engaging with potential micro-influencers
- [ ] **Email list building assessment**
  - How many subscribers? (Target: 50-100)
  - Is the popup/incentive converting? Adjust if < 2%
  - Send weekly newsletter (product + content mix)
- [ ] **Order fulfillment review**
  - Any shipping issues?
  - Delivery time acceptable?
  - Customer feedback on packaging?
  - Move winning products to US warehouse (if using China fulfillment)

**Time estimate: 8-10 hours/week**

## Growth: Weeks 9-12

### Week 9: Influencer Outreach

**Priority: Expand reach through partnerships**

- [ ] **Build influencer target list**
  - 20-30 micro-influencers (5K-50K followers)
  - Eco/sustainability niche on Instagram and TikTok
  - Check engagement rates (want >3%)
- [ ] **Begin engagement**
  - Follow and genuinely engage with content (2 weeks before pitching)
  - Comment thoughtfully, share their content
- [ ] **Send outreach messages**
  - Personalized DM or email
  - Offer: Free products + unique discount code (10% commission)
  - Creative brief but creative freedom
  - Target 3-5 partnerships in first batch
- [ ] **Prepare influencer kits**
  - Product selection (2-3 products per influencer)
  - Brand info sheet
  - Unique discount code per influencer
  - Suggested content angles (not scripts)

**Time estimate: 6-8 hours**

### Week 10: Email Automation

**Priority: Set up automated revenue-generating flows**

- [ ] **Build abandoned cart flow**
  - Email 1: 1 hour after abandonment (reminder)
  - Email 2: 24 hours (social proof)
  - Email 3: 72 hours (5% off incentive)
  - Test flow with a test cart abandonment
- [ ] **Build post-purchase flow**
  - Email 1: Order confirmation (already exists)
  - Email 2: Shipping notification (build when integration ready)
  - Email 3: Day 14 - Review request
  - Email 4: Day 30 - Restock suggestion (consumables)
- [ ] **Build welcome series**
  - Email 1: Welcome + 10% discount delivery
  - Email 2: Day 3 - Brand story + mission
  - Email 3: Day 7 - Top eco tips + product recommendations
- [ ] **Set up regular newsletter cadence**
  - Tuesday sends (best open rates for e-commerce)
  - Alternate: Product spotlight, educational content, promo

**Time estimate: 8-12 hours (one-time setup)**

### Week 11: Content & SEO Push

**Priority: Build organic traffic foundation**

- [ ] **Audit blog performance**
  - Which posts rank? Which get traffic?
  - What keywords are you appearing for?
  - Identify content gaps
- [ ] **Create 3-5 cornerstone content pieces**
  - Comprehensive guides (2,000-3,000 words)
  - "Ultimate Guide to Eco-Friendly Kitchen Products"
  - "Best Coffee Accessories for Home Baristas 2026"
  - "Zero-Waste Bathroom: Complete Starter Guide"
- [ ] **Internal linking strategy**
  - Every blog post links to relevant products
  - Every product page links to related blog content
  - Category pages link to buying guides
- [ ] **Pinterest acceleration**
  - Should have 200+ pins by now
  - Create pin templates in Canva for efficiency
  - Test Video Pins for higher engagement
  - Join relevant group boards if available

**Time estimate: 8-10 hours**

### Week 12: Review & Plan Month 4+

**Priority: Assess performance and plan next phase**

- [ ] **Comprehensive metrics review**
  - Total revenue (target: $2,000-3,000 conservative)
  - Total orders (target: 50-75)
  - Average order value
  - Customer acquisition cost by channel
  - Conversion rate
  - Email subscriber count (target: 100-200)
  - Top 5 selling products
  - Bottom 5 products (candidates for removal)
- [ ] **Channel performance assessment**
  - Google Shopping ROAS
  - Pinterest traffic and conversions
  - Instagram growth and engagement
  - Blog organic traffic trend
  - Email open/click rates
- [ ] **Financial review**
  - Revenue vs projections from [06-FINANCIAL-MODEL.md](06-FINANCIAL-MODEL.md)
  - Actual gross margins vs projected
  - Marketing spend efficiency
  - Cash flow positive yet?
- [ ] **Decision point**
  - Continue with current strategy? (On track)
  - Pivot product focus? (Data shows different winners)
  - Increase ad budget? (If ROAS > 3x)
  - Start direct sourcing? (If products consistently sell)
- [ ] **Plan Month 4-6 priorities**
  - Products to add/remove
  - Marketing budget adjustments
  - Influencer partnership expansion
  - Direct sourcing timeline for top sellers
  - Meta Ads test launch (if Google Shopping profitable)
  - Consider seasonal promotions (if approaching holiday)

**Time estimate: 4-6 hours**

## Emergency Playbook

### If No Sales After 4 Weeks
1. Check Google Analytics - is traffic reaching the site?
2. If no traffic: Increase ad spend, post more on social
3. If traffic but no sales: Product pages need work (photos, copy, pricing)
4. If add-to-carts but no checkouts: Checkout friction issue, test the flow
5. Ask 5 people to attempt a purchase and give feedback

### If High Returns/Complaints
1. Pull the problematic product immediately
2. Contact supplier about quality issues
3. If systemic: Switch suppliers
4. If product-specific: Remove from catalog
5. Turn complaints into content ("We listened and improved...")

### If Ad Spend Isn't Converting
1. Pause all ads for 3 days (save budget)
2. Review: Are product titles/images optimized for shopping ads?
3. Check: Is the landing page (product page) compelling?
4. Test: Lower prices on 2-3 products temporarily
5. Restart with $3/day on top 5 products only

### If a Product Goes Viral
1. Immediately check supplier inventory
2. Order additional samples to verify quality at scale
3. Increase ad budget on that product specifically
4. Create content around it (blog post, social series)
5. Contact supplier about bulk pricing
6. Add related products / bundle opportunities
