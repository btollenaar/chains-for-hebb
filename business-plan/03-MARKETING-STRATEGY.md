## Organic Channels

### Pinterest (Highest Priority Organic Channel)

**Why Pinterest First:**
- Visual search engine (not social media) - content lives for months, not hours
- #1 platform for home decor/organization/eco-living content
- 450M+ monthly active users, 80% discover new brands on Pinterest
- Users come with purchase intent (unlike Instagram where they scroll passively)
- Eco/sustainable living is a top trending category

**Pinterest Strategy:**

**Account Setup:**
- Business account with claimed website
- Rich Pins enabled (automatically pulls product data)
- Product catalog synced for Shopping Pins

**Pin Types:**
| Pin Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Product Pins | 3-5/day | Direct product discovery |
| Lifestyle/Aesthetic Pins | 2-3/day | Brand building, inspiration |
| Tips/Infographic Pins | 1-2/day | Educational value, saves |
| Blog post Pins | 1-2/day | Drive traffic to content |
| Video Pins | 2-3/week | Product demos, unboxings |

**Board Strategy:**
- "Eco-Friendly Kitchen Essentials"
- "Zero-Waste Bathroom Ideas"
- "Sustainable Gift Ideas"
- "Coffee Lover's Corner"
- "Green Cleaning Tips"
- "Eco Home Organization"
- "Sustainable Living 101"
- "Tea Time Rituals"

**Content Style:**
- Bright, clean photography with natural light
- Lifestyle shots showing products in use
- Before/after comparisons (plastic vs eco alternative)
- Infographics (e.g., "5 Swaps to Reduce Kitchen Plastic")
- Text overlay Pins with tips/stats

**Growth Targets:**
| Month | Followers | Monthly Impressions | Monthly Clicks |
|-------|-----------|--------------------|----|
| 1 | 50-100 | 5,000-10,000 | 50-100 |

### TikTok (Observation → Testing)

**Phase 1:** Study successful eco-friendly and coffee TikTok accounts. Save ideas.

**Phase 2:** Start posting 2-3x/week:
- Quick eco swaps (30 seconds)
- Product unboxings
- "Things you didn't know were bad for the environment"
- Coffee/tea preparation ASMR
- Satisfying packaging/unboxing content

**Why Wait:** TikTok requires consistent video production. Focus on Pinterest and Instagram first where images (lower effort) work well.

## Paid Advertising

### Google Shopping Ads (Primary Paid Channel)

**Why Google Shopping:**
- Highest-intent traffic (people searching for specific products)
- Average CPC for shopping ads: $0.66 (vs $1-3 for search ads)
- Visual product listings with price, image, reviews
- Product feed integrates with your existing catalog

**Budget Progression:**
| Phase | Daily Budget | Monthly Budget | Expected ROAS |
|-------|-------------|---------------|---------------|
| Phase 1 (test) | $5-10/day | $150-300 | 1.5-2.5x |
| Phase 2 (scale) | $15-25/day | $450-750 | 2.5-4x |
| Phase 3 (optimize) | $25-50/day | $750-1,500 | 3-5x |

**Campaign Structure:**
```
Smart Shopping Campaign (broad test)
├── All Products (initial test)
│
Then split into:
├── Campaign 1: Eco Kitchen (highest margin products)
├── Campaign 2: Eco Cleaning & Personal Care
├── Campaign 3: Coffee & Tea Accessories
└── Campaign 4: Gift Sets & Bundles
```

**Product Feed Optimization:**
- Title format: "[Brand] + [Product] + [Key Feature] + [Size/Count]"
  - Example: "EcoNest Beeswax Food Wraps - Reusable, Organic Cotton, 3-Pack"
- Include "eco-friendly", "sustainable", "reusable" in titles
- High-quality white-background product images
- Competitive pricing visible in listing
- Accurate GTIN/MPN when available

**Key Metrics to Track:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Click-through rate (CTR) | > 1.5% |
| Cost per click (CPC) | < $0.80 |
| Conversion rate | > 2% |
| Return on ad spend (ROAS) | > 3x |
| Cost per acquisition (CPA) | < $15 |

### Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Ads - Phase 2

**Budget:** Start at $10/day in Phase 2

**Campaign Types:**
1. **Lookalike audiences** from email list (once you have 100+ customers)
2. **Interest targeting:** Eco-friendly living, sustainability, zero waste, specialty coffee
3. **Retargeting:** Website visitors who didn't purchase (highest ROAS)

**Ad Formats:**
- Carousel ads showing product collections
- Video ads (15-30 second product demos)
- Collection ads with catalog integration

**Expected Performance:**
| Metric | Estimate |
|--------|----------|
| CPM (cost per 1,000 impressions) | $8-15 |
| CPC | $0.50-1.50 |
| CTR | 1-2% |
| Conversion rate | 1-3% |
| ROAS target | > 2.5x |

### Automated Email Flows

**Flow 1: Welcome Series (3 emails)**
| Email | Timing | Subject | Goal |
|-------|--------|---------|------|
| 1 | Immediately | "Welcome! Here's your 10% off" | Deliver discount, introduce brand |
| 2 | Day 3 | "Our Mission: A Greener Home" | Brand story, values |
| 3 | Day 7 | "Top 5 Eco Swaps to Start Today" | Value content + product links |

**Flow 2: Abandoned Cart (3 emails)**
| Email | Timing | Subject | Goal |
|-------|--------|---------|------|
| 1 | 1 hour | "You left something behind!" | Remind, show cart items |
| 2 | 24 hours | "Still thinking about it?" | Social proof, reviews |
| 3 | 72 hours | "Last chance: 5% off your cart" | Small incentive to close |

**Flow 3: Post-Purchase (4 emails)**
| Email | Timing | Subject | Goal |
|-------|--------|---------|------|
| 1 | Immediately | "Order confirmed!" | Confirmation + what to expect |
| 2 | Day 3-5 | "Your order is on its way!" | Tracking info + anticipation |
| 3 | Day 14 | "How are you enjoying your [product]?" | Request review |
| 4 | Day 30 | "Time to restock?" | Repeat purchase prompt (consumables) |

**Flow 4: Win-Back (2 emails)**
| Email | Timing | Subject | Goal |
|-------|--------|---------|------|
| 1 | 60 days no purchase | "We miss you! Here's what's new" | Re-engage with new products |
| 2 | 90 days no purchase | "15% off to welcome you back" | Stronger incentive |

## Influencer Marketing

### Micro-Influencer Strategy (Phase 2-3)

**Why Micro-Influencers:**
- 1,000-50,000 followers
- 3-8% engagement rates (vs 1-2% for larger accounts)
- Affordable: $100-500 per post or product gifting
- Authentic recommendations trusted by followers

**Target Influencer Profiles:**
| Type | Platform | Followers | Engagement | Compensation |
|------|----------|-----------|------------|-------------|
| Eco/Zero-Waste Blogger | Instagram | 5K-25K | 4-6% | Product gift + $100-200 |
| Sustainable Living Creator | TikTok | 10K-50K | 5-8% | Product gift + $150-300 |
| Home Organization | Pinterest | 10K-30K | N/A (saves) | Product gift + $100 |
| Coffee Enthusiast | Instagram/YouTube | 5K-20K | 4-7% | Product gift + $100-200 |
| Mom/Family Lifestyle | Instagram | 10K-50K | 3-5% | Product gift + $150-250 |

**Outreach Process:**
1. Create target list of 20-30 influencers
2. Engage with their content for 2 weeks (genuine comments, shares)
3. Send personalized DM or email with collaboration proposal
4. Offer: Free products + commission (10% affiliate code) OR flat fee
5. Provide creative brief but allow creative freedom
6. Track performance with unique discount codes per influencer

**Expected Results per Micro-Influencer:**
| Metric | Conservative | Moderate | Optimistic |
|--------|-------------|----------|------------|
| Impressions | 5,000 | 15,000 | 30,000 |
| Link clicks | 50 | 150 | 400 |
| Conversions | 2-3 | 5-8 | 10-15 |
| Revenue | $50-75 | $125-200 | $250-375 |
| CAC | $33-50 | $13-20 | $7-10 |

**Budget Allocation:**
- Phase 2: $300-500 total (3-5 influencers)
- Phase 3: $500-1,000/month (scale winning partnerships)

## Path to First 100 Customers

| Milestone | Strategy | Timeline |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Customers 1-10 | Friends, family, personal network | Week 1-2 |
| Customers 11-25 | Instagram/Pinterest launch + first paid ads | Week 3-6 |
| Customers 26-50 | Blog traffic begins, email captures convert | Week 7-10 |
| Customers 51-75 | Google Shopping optimization, first influencer | Week 11-14 |
| Customers 76-100 | Email automation, returning customers, organic SEO | Week 15-18 |

**Key Insight:** The first 10-25 customers are the hardest. Lean into personal network and small targeted ads to get initial data and reviews. After 25 customers with positive reviews, the flywheel starts turning.
